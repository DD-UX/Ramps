# Assignment Brief (Silver.dev)

> Source: https://you.ashbyhq.com/Silver/assignment/d7093ff1-0d78-448b-9d7c-f6cc963ea7fd

## Build a payables product

Use a modern accounts payable product like **Ramp Bill Pay** as inspiration.

Reference: https://support.ramp.com/hc/en-us/articles/27579228841875-Managing-bills-and-payments-on-Bill-Pay

Your job is to quickly understand the product, decide what the core workflows are,
scope a sensible MVP, and build it end-to-end.

**We care as much about what you build as how you build it.**

## What to submit

- A working product: data models, backend, frontend, everything.
- A short README covering:
  - what you think the product does
  - the workflows you prioritized
  - what you left out and why
  - setup instructions
  - key architecture / data model decisions

## What we're evaluating

- product taste
- design/ui
- scope judgment
- UX quality
- ability to grok complex workflows and business use cases
- simple, robust systems
- raw output — how much can you get done in a short amount of time
- features you implement

## Design engineer track (applies to us)

> If you are applying as a design engineer, please **reproduce and implement the
> Ramp design system** in your finished product, based on what you can gather
> online and through various sources. It should look and feel like Ramp. Come
> equipped with opinions on where things can be improved, but the baseline is
> the Ramp design.

## Notes

- make reasonable assumptions where details are ambiguous
- use realistic demo data
- optimize for clarity and judgment
